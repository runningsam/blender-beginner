How can this PDF guide help you?

Hello everyone! I am Sandra Kolar or as you may know me from my other social media,SaTales.

I started to publish YouTube Blender tutorials, tips and tricks for fun, but when I realized howmuch people enjoy them, I decided to take it all to the next level and write a real guide for allbeginners.

I often get messages and comments that there are not many tutorials for those who are juststarting their 3D modeling journey and opening Blender for the first time.

I was also once a beginner, and I know how hard it was for me to learn 3D without anyprevious knowledge.

So this ebook is for all of you who want to learn Blender, but don't know how and where tostart.

I hope you will enjoy learning with me and that this book will help you in your 3D journey.

Now, it is time to start with our lessons!

Have fun everyone!

тАЛWhat is the conclusion?тАЛ
тАЛIf you duplicate the object with тАЬSHIFT+DтАЭ number of your polygons will increase, but you willтАЛ
тАЛhave separated objects and whatever you do on other object it wonтАЩt be replicated to yourтАЛ
тАЛduplicated object automatically.тАЛ
тАЛIf you duplicate the object with тАЬALT+DтАЭ number of your polygons will stay the same, but youтАЛ
тАЛwill have connected objects and whatever you do on other objects in the edit mode, it  will beтАЛ
тАЛreplicated to your duplicated object automatically. But if you move, scale or rotate your objectтАЛ
тАЛin the object mode, it wonтАЩt affect the other object.тАЛ
тАЛIf you change colors, your other objects will change the color as well.тАЛ

тАЛor edit in edit mode?тАЛ

тАЛrotation or a scale), you will get this messageтАЛ

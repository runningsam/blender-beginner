How can this e-book help you? 
Hello everyone! I am Sandra Kolar,  though you might know me as SaTales on social media. 
I started publishing YouTube Blender tutorials, tips, and tricks for fun, but when I realized 
how much people were enjoying them, I decided to take it all to the next level and write a 
real guide for all beginners. 
I often receive messages and comments that there are not many tutorials for those who are 
just starting their 3D modeling journey and opening Blender for the first time. 
I was also once a beginner, and I know how hard it was for me to learn 3D without any 
previous knowledge. 
This ebook is for all of you who want to learn Blender, but don't know how or where to start. 
I hope you will enjoy learning with me and that this book will help you in your 3D journey. 
Now, it is time to start with our lessons! 
Have fun, everyone!
